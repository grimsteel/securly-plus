Securly Plus

Thank you for installing Securly Plus!

Securly Flex will now use your system theme, and will show the "Today + 4" view by default. We've also applied numerous performance improvements. You can customize these settings on the options page.

Warning: By default, you'll have to enter a search query in order to see a list of available sessions. This is by design, as it saves you from having to wait for the entire list of sessions to load. You can disable this feature on the options page!
